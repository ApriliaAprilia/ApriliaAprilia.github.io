Hi, my name is Aprilia :)

I am a young researcher working on the processing and characterization of metal alloys. I am a researcher in Assoc Professor Zhou Wei's group in Rolls-Royce@NTU Corporate Laboratory, Singapore. My research interest is in remanufacturing of engineering components for aerospace and marine and offshore industry. I received my PhD in 2021 under the supervision of Assoc Professor Tor Shu Beng and Assoc Professor Seet Gim Lee, Gerald in Singapore Centre for 3D Printing, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore.
