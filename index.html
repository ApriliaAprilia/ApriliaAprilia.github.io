Hi, my name is Aprilia :)

I am a researcher focused on the processing and characterization of metal alloys, with strong interests in advanced manufacturing technologies, particularly additive manufacturing and materials characterization. I am highly motivated, disciplined, and collaborative, with a strong drive for continuously learning. I seek to grow through mentorship from experienced researchers, engineers, and technologists while making meaningful contributions to the advancements of engineering technologies.
I am currently working as a research fellow in Assoc Prof Zhou Wei's group in Singapore Centre for 3D Printing, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore.
