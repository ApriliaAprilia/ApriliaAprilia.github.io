Biography

I am currently a final year PhD student in the Singapore Centre for 3D Printing, School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My research interest is in 3D model reconstruction of damaged parts for an automated remanufacturing process using additive manufacturing technology. I am pursuing my PhD under the supervision of Assoc Professor Tor Shu Beng and Assoc Professor Seet Gim Lee, Gerald. I received my Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from NTU in 2016.
